How to Play Poker

Welcome to the game! Here are the basics:

Each player is dealt two private cards.

Five public cards are placed face-up on the table.

Your goal is to make the best five-card hand using any mix of your two cards and the five public cards.

Game Actions

Check - stay in the game without betting more.

Fold - give up the round. You lose less money than betting and losing.

Winning

The player with the best hand wins the pot (the money bet in that round).

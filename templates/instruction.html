How to Play Poker

Welcome to the poker game! Here are the instructions:

Each player is dealt two private cards, and five public cards are dealt face-up on the table.

Your goal is to make the best five-card hand using any combination of your two cards and the five public cards.

You can choose to "Raise" to increase the bet, "Check" to stay in the game without raising, or "Fold" to forfeit the round.

If you fold, you will lost less money than other options.

The player with the best hand wins the pot.

Hand rankings (from highest to lowest): Royal Stright Flush, Straight Flush, Four of a Kind, Full House, Flush, Straight, Three of a Kind, Two Pair, One Pair, High Card.

Good luck and have fun!

Notice: If you quit the playing page during a game, the game will continue with you check to the end! And you may lost money this way!
